The Magic Jar is a simple self-care idea where each colored chit contains a short quote from the Bhagavad Gita related to different emotions like calmness, anger, anxiety, and positivity. whenever you feel a certain emotion, you pick a chit from the jar and read it to gain clarity, peace or motivation.It is designed to help manage emotions, stay mindful and build a positive mindset in a simple and meaningful way.

Live at: https://bikrant07.github.io/magic-jar/
